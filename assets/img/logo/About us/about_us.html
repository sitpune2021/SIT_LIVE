About Us

Welcome to CukNuk, where innovation meets tradition in the world of frozen foods. Founded in 2020 as a family-owned business, CukNuk has grown into a global leader in frozen food manufacturing, proudly crafting exceptional products in India and delivering them to customers worldwide.
Our passion lies in redefining frozen foods by creating healthy, flavorful, and convenient meal solutions. At the heart of our operations is a commitment to sustainability, quality, and health. We produce a diverse range of products that are free from chemicals, artificial colors, and additives, ensuring that every bite is wholesome and nourishing.
